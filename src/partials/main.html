How to Play

Simple to Learn, Hard to Master!

Match 3+ gems of the same type to eliminate them

Match 4+ to trigger special combos and explosive boosts

Complete level objectives like breaking ice blocks, freeing trapped gems, or collecting treasures

Plan your moves to earn stars and unlock new magical worlds

Use your boosts wisely to crush even the most challenging levels!

Power-Ups

  • Rainbow Gem

    Clears all gems of a single color

  • Bomb Jewel

    Explodes surrounding tiles

  • Lightning Strike

    Clears full rows or columns

  • Combo Blaster

    Activated by combining two power-ups for massive effects

  • Treasure Crate
