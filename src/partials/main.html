Game Description

Step into a shimmering world of jewel-matching magic! Jewel Quest Mystic Legend takes match-3 gameplay to a new level with 1,000+ exciting levels, daily rewards, global leaderboards, and endless sparkle-filled fun. Perfect for all ages, this relaxing yet challenging puzzle adventure offers a captivating blend of logic, strategy, and charm.

Whether you're playing on the bus or relaxing at home, this offline-friendly gem quest is your go-to source of satisfaction and fun!

How to Play

Simple to Learn, Hard to Master!

Match 3+ gems of the same type to eliminate them

Match 4+ to trigger special combos and explosive boosts

Complete level objectives like breaking ice blocks, freeing trapped gems, or collecting treasures

Plan your moves to earn stars and unlock new magical worlds

Use your boosts wisely to crush even the most challenging levels!
